# Rage Platformer sound-effect provenance

`sfx_life_lost.wav` and `sfx_door_enter.wav` are generated specifically for
this project by `Assets/Editor/RageDeathAndAudioUpgrade.cs`.

They are synthesized from mathematical oscillators and deterministic noise.
No third-party recording, sample pack, voice, melody, or downloaded audio is
included. Therefore, there is no attribution requirement originating from
third-party audio inputs used to create these files. They were made for use in
this project, including its commercial releases.
